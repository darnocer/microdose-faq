# What is "microdosing"?

It's important to note that "microdosing" is not a standard term, and some people may use the term to describe a dose that would result in a small trip.

For the purposes of this site, "microdosing" refers to ingesting an extremely small dose of a psychedelic, typically psilocybin or LSD, that will produce subtle cognitive changes without significantly altering perception.
