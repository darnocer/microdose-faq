# What are the negative effects or side effects of microdosing?

<Callout type="warning">`Todo:: cite sources`</Callout>

In the Thomas Anderson study, the top 3 most commonly reported challenges were:

- Concerns about legality (this ranked first by a significant margin)
- Physical Discomfort, such as upset stomach
- "Other Concerns" about the long-term consequences of microdosing, due to lack of scientific research

In general, the most commonly reported side effects from microdosing includes:

- Muscle cramps
- Upset stomach
- Dizziness
- Anxiety
- Overstimulation
- Irritability or agitation on "off" days
- Paranoia (related to the legality)
