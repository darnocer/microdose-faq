# Why would someone want to microdose?

The practice of microdosing has been gaining popularity due to increasing anecdotal reports of positive benefits. Reasons people may choose to microdose include to increase creativity, manage symptoms of anxiety or depression, improve emotional processing, and enhance athletic performance, to name a few.

Additionally, psychedelic research is showing promising potential of microdosing for the treatment of:

- Alcoholism
- Addiction
- Depression & Anxiety
- PTSD
- End-of-life anxiety
