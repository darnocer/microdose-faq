# Why would someone want to microdose?

There are many reasons why someone might choose to microdose. Many people choose to microdose to increase creativity, manage symptoms of anxiety or depression, or improve emotional processing, as examples. The practice of microdosing has been gaining popularity due to its potential to offer new perspectives on emotional challenges and as a holistic approach to healing.

Additionally, psychedelic research is showing promising potential of microdosing for the treatment of:
* Alcoholism
* Addiction
* Depression & Anxiety
* PTSD
* End-of-life anxiety
